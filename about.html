About Us

We are a recognizable club to help, mentor students majoring in disciplines related to technology. To help students build their professional and personal networks, get access to Microsoft resources, and work together to build solutions for worldwide problems.

We take workshops and meetups to help students learn new technologies. We support them to become Microsoft Student Partner, which is world recognized community where they get much more exposure to tech world
